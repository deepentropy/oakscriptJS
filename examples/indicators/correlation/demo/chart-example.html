OakScriptJS - Correlation Indicator

Correlation calculates the Pearson correlation coefficient between two data series. Values range from -1 (perfect negative correlation) to +1 (perfect positive correlation).

How it works:

The correlation indicator measures how two price series move together:

  • +1: Perfect positive correlation (move together)
  • 0: No correlation (independent movement)
  • -1: Perfect negative correlation (move oppositely)
